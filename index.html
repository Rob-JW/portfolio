About

I’m a British Army Warrant Officer with 20+ years of experience in defence. I’m now learning cyber security – applying engineering discipline, reliability fundamentals, and hands-on experimentation to blue-team and offensive security.

I have recently gained the CompTIA Security+ and ISO 9001 qualifications and I’m actively studying for the CREST Practitioner Security Analyst (CPSA). This website is my first project in building a portfolio of labs, write-ups, and small tools that bridge the gap between my engineering background and my future career in cyber security.
